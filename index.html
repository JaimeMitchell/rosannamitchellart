Art Workshops in Guilford, Connecticut

Rosanna Mitchell is a mixed media artist based in Guilford, CT. Her layered botanical works are held in private collections and featured in galleries across Connecticut, California, and Wyoming. Collect original artwork or join a seasonal mixed media workshop in Guilford designed for artists of all experience levels.
