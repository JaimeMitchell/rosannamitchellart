Join mixed media and botanical art workshops in Guilford, Connecticut. These seasonal art classes are designed for beginners through experienced artists and explore layered techniques, color development, and botanical compositiontechniques in a supportive studio environment. In the summer Rosanna's workshops focus on ecoprinting and ice-dye techniques on clothing and textiles.

Mixed Media and Botanical Art Classes in Guilford CT

Workshops and private classes are held in Guilford, CT and serve artists throughout the Connecticut shoreline and New Haven County.

Participants develop skills in texture, color layering, and expressive botanical design while creating finished mixed media artwork. Small class sizes allow for individualized instruction and hands-on guidance.
