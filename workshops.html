Join mixed media and botanical art workshops in Guilford, Connecticut led by artist Rosanna Mitchell. These seasonal art classes are designed for beginners through experienced artists and explore layered techniques, color development, and botanical composition.

Mixed Media and Botanical Art Classes in Guilford CT

Workshops are held in Guilford, CT and serve artists throughout the Connecticut shoreline and New Haven County. Each session focuses on building confidence with watercolor, ink, acrylic, handmade papers, and collage techniques in a supportive studio environment.

Participants develop skills in texture, color layering, and expressive botanical design while creating finished mixed media artwork. Small class sizes allow for individualized instruction and hands-on guidance.
